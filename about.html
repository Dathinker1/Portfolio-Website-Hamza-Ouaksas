Hi, I’m Hamza Ouaksas, an illustrator and concept artist based in the Netherlands. From a young age, I’ve been captivated by the (visual) world and its intricate mechanisms, always seeking to immerse myself in vibrant, lively universes. This fascination has remained a driving force behind my passion for creating and expressing captivating stories in my art.

One of my strong points is being able to percevere when faced with difficult problems. My natural love for exploring and problemsolving keeps me challenged and curious, while my communication skills and flexibility make me relieable to work with. I’ve always had a keen eye for subtle details and hidden narratives in the world around me. These small, often overlooked elements inspire me and spark creativity in my work, allowing me to create art that draws others in and invites curiosity.

When I’m not creating, I enjoy playing games with friends, playing the piano, or embarking on long hikes across Holland and beyond.
